Stop Conditions

  • Do not prototype a credential-bearing profile app before the Keychain/redaction convention exists.
  • Do not prioritize a web wrapper whose durable state lives only in IndexedDB.
  • Do not ship a write-enabled database wrapper before read-only mode and backup/export behavior are explicit.
  • Do not use a proprietary web app as embedded runtime without a clear embedding license.
